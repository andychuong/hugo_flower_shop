How to Care for Your Roses

Roses are one of the most beloved flowers, and with proper care, they can last for over a week. Here are our top tips for keeping your roses fresh and beautiful.

Water is Key

  • Change the water every 2-3 days
  • Use lukewarm water
  • Add flower food if provided
  • Trim stems at an angle every few days

Location Matters

  • Keep roses away from direct sunlight
  • Avoid placing near heat sources
  • Keep away from fruit (ethylene gas can shorten their life)

Temperature

Roses prefer cooler temperatures. Keep them in a room that’s between 65-72°F (18-22°C).

With these simple tips, your roses should stay beautiful for 7-10 days!
